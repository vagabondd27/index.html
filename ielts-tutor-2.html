✍️ Writing Task 1 — Cách viết Introduction
Công thức Introduction Task 1:

The [chart/graph/table/diagram] [shows/illustrates/presents] [thông tin gì] [ở đâu/khi nào].
Ví dụ đề bài: "The bar chart shows the percentage of students using different transport to school in 2020."

Cách paraphrase (KHÔNG copy y chang đề):
→ "The given bar chart illustrates the proportion of pupils who commuted to school via various means of transport in 2020."

❌ Sai: "The bar chart shows the percentage of students..."
✅ Đúng: Paraphrase ít nhất 3-4 từ khác nhau

Cấu trúc câu trả lời Part 1 (PEEL):

Academic Word List — Hover để xem nghĩa
analyse
verb
phân tích
"The study analyses the impact of social media on teenagers."
analyse
to examine in detail → examine, investigate, study
significant
adjective
đáng kể, quan trọng
"There has been a significant increase in online shopping."
significant
large/important enough to notice → notable, considerable, substantial
indicate
verb
chỉ ra, cho thấy
"The graph indicates a sharp rise in temperatures."
indicate
to show or suggest → suggest, demonstrate, reveal
contrast
verb / noun
tương phản, so sánh khác biệt
"In contrast, urban areas experienced rapid growth."
contrast
to compare differences → by contrast, on the contrary
demonstrate
verb
chứng minh, thể hiện
"The data demonstrates a clear trend towards remote work."
demonstrate
to clearly show → show, prove, illustrate
proportion
noun
tỷ lệ, phần
"A large proportion of students prefer online learning."
proportion
a part in relation to whole → percentage, fraction, share
